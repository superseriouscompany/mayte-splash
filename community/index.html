Application Review

We aim to foster a diverse community of creative and unique individuals. All applications are reviewed in the order in which they are received.

Who We Are

    Applicants are admitted based upon the following criteria:
  • Civility Does the applicant carry themself with an air of humilty and respect for the world around them? Do the submitted materials reflect an open-mindedness compatible with Unicorn's mission for diversity?
  • Identity How will the applicant stand out uniquely within the Unicorn community? Does the applicant demonstrate creativity and take pride in their work and lifestyle?
  • Influence Does the applicant appear to be a positive influence within their current social sphere? This is less about communal clout and followers than how they engage with others on-/offline.
